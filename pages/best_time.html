Best Time to Visit Indian States

January
  • Rajasthan Pleasant desert weather for exploring Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur
  • Goa Perfect beach season & festivals like Sunburn
  • Gujarat Rann Utsav in full swing in the white desert
February
  • Kerala Cool weather, perfect for houseboat cruises & hill stations
  • Maharashtra Visit Mumbai, Pune, and Ajanta-Ellora before summer heat
  • Odisha Explore Konark, Puri, and tribal regions comfortably
March
  • Uttar Pradesh Celebrate Holi in Mathura & Vrindavan
  • Karnataka Perfect time for Hampi, Coorg, and Chikmagalur
  • Madhya Pradesh Wildlife safaris in national parks like Kanha, Bandhavgarh
April
  • Andaman & Nicobar Crystal clear water, scuba diving & fewer crowds
  • Tamil Nadu Temple towns & early summer hill stations like Ooty
  • Sikkim Blooming rhododendrons, scenic mountain drives
May
  • Ladakh Roads reopen, stunning mountain views & culture
  • Uttarakhand Visit Kedarnath, Rishikesh, Nainital in cool weather
  • Manipur Lush landscapes & cultural festivals
June
  • Kashmir Alpine valleys, snowcapped views, perfect for honeymoon
  • Himachal Pradesh Rohtang, Spiti Valley & apple orchards
  • Meghalaya Waterfalls, living root bridges, and monsoon beauty
July
  • Coorg, Karnataka Monsoon magic with coffee plantations & waterfalls
  • Kerala Romantic rains, lush green backwaters
  • Maharashtra Visit Lonavala, Malshej Ghat during the greenest season
August
  • Wayanad, Kerala Verdant landscapes, romantic weather
  • Chikmagalur Coffee estates soaked in monsoon beauty
  • Assam Majuli Island & Kaziranga (off-season peace)
September
  • Udaipur, Rajasthan Green lakesides post-monsoon
  • Valley of Flowers, Uttarakhand Peak bloom season for alpine flowers
  • Ziro Valley, Arunachal Home to the Ziro Music Festival
October
  • Jaipur & Jodhpur Cooler temps, start of festive season
  • Varanasi Experience Dev Deepawali & spiritual Ganga aarti
  • Sundarbans, West Bengal Best time for wildlife spotting
November
  • Pushkar, Rajasthan Colorful Camel Fair & cultural buzz
  • Gokarna, Karnataka Tranquil beaches with perfect weather
  • Khajuraho Heritage temples in post-monsoon beauty
December
  • Goa Christmas & New Year party capital
  • Auli, Uttarakhand Snow sports & skiing season starts
  • Kolkata Festive streets and cultural warmth
