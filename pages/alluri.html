Alluri Sitarama Raju

Indian Freedom Fighter | Leader of Rampa Rebellion

Welcome to the Legacy of Alluri Sitarama Raju

A valiant leader who fought against British oppression in India’s forests.

Key Facts

  • Full Name: Alluri Sitarama Raju
  • Born: 4 July 1897, Pandrangi, Andhra Pradesh
  • Known as: Rampa Leader
  • Philosophy: Courage, Resistance, Justice
  • Occupation: Tribal Leader, Freedom Fighter

Historical Significance

  • Leader of the Rampa Rebellion (1922–1924) against British rule.
  • Defended tribal rights and forest lands from British exploitation.
  • Symbol of courage and resistance in Andhra Pradesh.
  • Inspired future freedom fighters in India’s independence struggle.

Philosophy

  • Believed in armed struggle as a means to protect his people.
  • Encouraged tribal unity and self-reliance.
  • Promoted resistance against oppression and injustice.
  • Legacy continues to inspire bravery and patriotism.

Memorials & Legacy

  • Statues and memorials in Andhra Pradesh in his honor.
  • Remembered for bravery in fighting British colonial forces.
  • Rampa region celebrates his contributions annually.
  • Recognized as a hero of tribal and Indian resistance movements.
