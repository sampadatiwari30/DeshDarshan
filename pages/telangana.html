Telangana Uncovered: Fast Facts

Telangana became India’s 29th state in 2014 after its separation from Andhra Pradesh. 🗓️

Hyderabad, the capital, is a major IT hub often called “Cyberabad” 💻🌐

The Kakatiya Dynasty has left behind architectural marvels like the Ramappa Temple 🛕

Telangana is rich in forest reserves, with regions like Kawal and Eturnagaram Wildlife Sanctuaries 🌲🐘

The state is home to several tribal communities like the Lambadis and Gonds, with rich cultural heritage 👣

Telangana boasts a mix of modern and historical—like HITEC City and Qutb Shahi Tombs coexisting 🏙️⛲
