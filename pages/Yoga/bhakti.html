Bhakti Yoga, the Yoga of Devotion, is one of the four main paths of yoga in Indian philosophy. Rooted in the Bhagavad Gita and ancient devotional traditions, it emphasizes love, faith, and surrender to the Divine. This path encourages cultivating a deep emotional connection with God through prayer, chanting, singing, and service. Saints like Meerabai, Tulsidas, Chaitanya Mahaprabhu are key figures in the Bhakti movement.
