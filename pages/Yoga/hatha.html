Hatha Yoga is one of the oldest and most widely practiced branches of yoga, dating back to the 11th century. It was formalized in texts like the Hatha Yoga Pradipika by Swami Swatmarama. The word "Hatha" combines Ha (sun) and Tha (moon), symbolizing balance of energies. It emphasizes physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation to prepare the body and mind for higher states of consciousness.
