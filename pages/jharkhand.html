Beyond the Hills: Jharkhand's Facts

Jharkhand is one of the leading mineral-producing states in India, rich in coal, iron ore, and bauxite. ⛏️💎

Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand, is known as the **City of Waterfalls**. 💦🌿

The famous Birsa Munda was a freedom fighter and tribal leader born in Jharkhand. 🙌🪔

Betla National Park in Jharkhand is home to tigers, elephants, and diverse flora and fauna. 🐅🌲

The tribal art forms like Sohrai and Khovar paintings of Jharkhand are globally admired. 🎨🖌️

The Damodar River flows through Jharkhand and is vital to the region's coal belt. 🌊🛶
