Bhagat Singh

Revolutionary Freedom Fighter | Martyr of India’s Independence

Remembering Bhagat Singh

A fearless revolutionary who sacrificed his life for India’s freedom.

Key Facts

  • Full Name: Bhagat Singh
  • Born: 28 September 1907, Banga, Punjab
  • Known as: Shaheed Bhagat Singh
  • Occupation: Revolutionary, Freedom Fighter
  • Famous For: Fight against British colonial rule, Martyrdom

Historical Significance

  • Key figure in the Indian independence movement.
  • Founded revolutionary organizations and participated in anti-British activities.
  • Inspired generations of freedom fighters in India.

Legacy & Influence

  • Executed at age 23, becoming a symbol of courage and sacrifice.
  • Remembered as a youth icon in Indian history.
  • Martyrs’ memorials and stamps dedicated to him across India.
