Subhas Chandra Bose

Leader of Azad Hind Fauj | Visionary Patriot

Remembering Subhas Chandra Bose

The fearless leader who inspired millions with his call – “Give me blood, and I will give you freedom.”

Key Facts

  • Full Name: Subhas Chandra Bose
  • Born: 23 January 1897, Cuttack, Odisha
  • Known as: Netaji
  • Occupation: Freedom Fighter, Leader of Azad Hind Fauj (INA)
  • Famous For: Leading the Indian National Army (INA) and fighting for India’s independence from British rule

Historical Significance

  • Founded the Indian National Army to fight British forces during World War II.
  • Advocated complete independence and armed resistance against colonial rule.
  • Formed the Provisional Government of Free India in 1943.

Legacy & Influence

  • Remembered as a symbol of selfless patriotism and leadership.
  • Inspired future generations with his slogan “Jai Hind”.
  • Celebrated every year on 23rd January as Netaji Jayanti across India.
