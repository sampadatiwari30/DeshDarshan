Beyond the Hills: Sikkim's Facts

Sikkim is the least populous and second smallest state of India but rich in culture and nature. 🌿

The Kanchenjunga, India’s highest mountain peak, is located in Sikkim. 🏔️

Home to over 500 species of butterflies and rare Himalayan flora like the Rhododendron 🌺🦋

Sikkim was an independent kingdom until it merged with India in 1975. 👑🇮🇳

The state has the highest literacy rate among northeastern states. 🧠📚

Yumthang Valley in Sikkim is known as the “Valley of Flowers” of the East! 🌸
