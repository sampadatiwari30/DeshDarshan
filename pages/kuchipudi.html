Andhra Roots 🌾

Kuchipudi originated in a small village named Kuchipudi in Andhra Pradesh — blending drama and dance since the 10th century.

Dance on a Plate 🥁

One of Kuchipudi’s iconic acts features a dancer performing intricate steps on a brass plate — while balancing a pot on the head!

Bhakti & Storytelling 📜

Deeply rooted in Vaishnavism, Kuchipudi tells stories of Krishna and Rama with expressive acting and graceful movement.

From Brahmins to All 🎭

Kuchipudi was once performed only by male Brahmin troupes. Today, it is practiced worldwide by dancers of all genders.

Revival & Recognition 🌟

The 20th-century revival of Kuchipudi was led by Vedantam Lakshminarayana and others — helping it gain classical status across India.
