üìñ Origin & History

Kho Kho is one of the oldest traditional outdoor games of India, believed to have originated thousands of years ago. The game is popular for its speed, strategy, and teamwork. It was officially recognized in 1959 with the formation of the Kho Kho Federation of India. Played by chasing and tagging opponents, Kho Kho is considered a game that builds agility, reflexes, and coordination.

Today, Kho Kho is not just a rural pastime but also an internationally recognized sport. The Ultimate Kho Kho League was launched in India to promote the game professionally. Schools across India include it in physical education to develop quick reflexes and team spirit among students.
